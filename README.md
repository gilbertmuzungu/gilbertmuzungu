## Who I Am

I grew up with one question: *why do some businesses thrive while others — with the same resources — don't?*

The answer almost always comes down to how well they understand their own numbers.

That curiosity took me through a degree in **Economics and Statistics**, into the **Kenya National Bureau of Statistics** analyzing national-scale datasets that shaped government policy, and eventually into building a practice that helps businesses see what their data has been quietly trying to tell them.

I don't just build dashboards. I translate messy, incomplete, and overwhelming data into decisions that actually change outcomes — using **SQL**, **Power BI**, **Excel**, and increasingly, **AI-powered automation pipelines** built with Zapier and precision prompt engineering.

I work **full-time, contract, project-based, or fractional** — and I show up the same way regardless of the arrangement: with full focus and real accountability.

## Featured Projects

### ☕ [Daily Grind — Pricing & Margin Analysis](https://github.com/gilbertmuzungu/product-margin-analysis-sql-powerbi)
> *The Director of Operations raised an alarm: rising COGS was quietly killing margins — even as revenue looked stable.*

Built a full end-to-end pipeline: unified 3 years of transactional data (4,400+ orders) using SQL `UNION ALL`, cleaned null revenue values with `CASE` logic, joined across 3 tables, then built an interactive Power BI dashboard with a dynamic DAX `SWITCH` metric selector. Identified the exact products falling below a 30% gross margin threshold in Q3 2025 and delivered pricing recommendations.

`SQL` `Power BI` `DAX` `Data Cleaning` `Margin Analysis` `Business Recommendations`
